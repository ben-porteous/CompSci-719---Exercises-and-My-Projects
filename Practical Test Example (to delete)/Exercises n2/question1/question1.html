PTX: The World Tour

In celebration of their massive 'The World' tour, Grammy Award-winning a cappella maestros Pentatonix are heading down to NZ for a one-off, comeback concert at Auckland's Spark Arena this February 2020.

Bastille: Doom Days Tour

British pop superstars Bastille return to New Zealand at the beginning of next year on the Doom Days Tour, Part 2. Visiting Auckland on Thursday 23 January 2020, this tour will be fans’ first chance to hear the band’s third studio album Doom Days performed live.

Hugh Jackman: The Man. The Music. The Show.

Academy Award® nominated, Golden Globe, Grammy and Tony Award-winning performer, Hugh Jackman, is bringing his The Man. The Music. The Show. WORLD TOUR to Auckland this September.

Fleetwood Mac

Legendary, GRAMMY-award winning band Fleetwood Mac are heading back to Auckland for four shows this September (Thursday 12 September, Saturday 14 September, Monday 16 September, and Thursday 19 September).

Khalid: Free Spirit Tour

21-year old superstar Khalid is heading back to New Zealand shores for his Free Spirit Tour. With his Grammy-nominated debut album American Teen having been certified double Platinum and his new record Free Spirit finally out in the world, there’s no better time for fans down under to experience the El Paso singer’s magic.

Kiss: End of the Road World Tour

After an epic and storied 45-year career that launched an era of rock n roll legends, KISS announced that they will launch their final tour ever in 2019, appropriately named END OF THE ROAD.
